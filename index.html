About Me

My story as a designer started off when I was a kid crafting every single thing lying around my home. Ever since then I always had an eye to make things beautiful and useful.I am an engineer turned self taught designer with a few wise mentors who guided me along my journey. My toolkit includes wireframing, prototyping, user research, user testing and design software like Figma, Protopie and Adobe Illustrator. With over 2+ years of experience in the field, I have had the privilege of working on a variety of projects for startups and clients. I strive to design products that are not only visually appealing but also easy to use. When I am not designing I spend my time doodling, cooking pasta and brownies.
